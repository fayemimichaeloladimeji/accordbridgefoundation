    International Day of the girl at Comprehensive Secondary School Mgbuosiminim 2017

    Celebrating International Day of the Girl-Child 2017 to raise awareness about the unique struggles and discrimination that girls face globally - such as unlimited opportunities, limited

    access to education, and gender-based violence. Having an interactive session with the students at Comprehensive Secondary School Mgbuosiminim Port-Harcourt.























    International Day of the girl at Model Girls Secondary School Rumueme Port-Harcourt 2018

    Celebrating International Day of the girl-child 2017 with a strong call for action to break down barriers and create a supportive environment that EmPOWER girls and enables them to thrive and fulfill their potential. Interactive session with students @ Model Girls Secondary School Rumueme Port-Harcourt.























    Livelihood and empowerment training for less privileged women Chika community, Abuja 2019.

    The women were trained in the production of liquid soap, bleach and house cleaning disinfectant at community levels. They were also provided with business skills training, access to financial services and connections with peers.



















    Livelihood and Empowerment Programme for women of Aleyita community, a suburb in Abuja 2021

    The women were trained in the production of liquid soap, bleach and house cleaning disinfectant at community levels. They were also provided with business skills training, access to financial services and connections with peers.























    2022 Entrepreneurship development programme for Senior Secondary School students of the Government Girls Secondary School Orominieke, Port-Harcourt.

    The programme’s objective centred on assisting these young secondary school girls to discover and develop small business ideas through entrepreneurship development and skill acquisition sessions.
