    Managment Team

    Accord-Bridge Foundation’s Management Team is made up of professionals who have a strong background in planning, designing, and implementing development initiatives.
    Jane Douglas
    National Coordinator

    Jane Douglas has well-rounded ... work experience having served many years in both public and private sectors of the Nigerian economy. While her experience includes working in a for-profit environment, her passion is to use her background and education to facilitate the empowerment of women and girls for a better and self-sustaining living. She is an ardent philanthropist who has dedicated her time and resources to the empowerment of women and the girl-child across Nigeria. Ms. Douglas has supported and facilitated, for over a decade, the training of several women and girls in various community-driven skills acquisition programmes. Her experience in the field fueled by her passion for women's and girls’ empowerment, motivated her to pioneer the Accord-Bridge Foundation. She has a kind heart and, an open spirit that reaches out to everyone with which she comes in contact. She mentors several young women and girls.

    Peter Asquo Isangedihi
    Field Operations Manager

    A senior Public Servant with several years... of increased responsibility facilitating project implementations, support, and training in Public service. He has spent over seventeen years consistently implementing effective solutions both in the government circle and outside the work environment. Before joining the Foundation, Mr. Peter volunteered for community service for various organizations and did several field-based projects, including offering extra-mural tutoring to the less privileged in several communities across Nigeria. Peter is passionate about helping women get out of poverty and has sponsored many skills acquisition programmes for several young women. His philanthropic actions towards the eradication of poverty especially amongst the female folks which he says give him ‘inner joy’ has helped many young women live a better life. He is responsible for the coordination of all field operations for the Accord-Bridge Foundation.

    Geoffrey Akueche

During his spare time, he volunteers for organizations with a mission to end poverty. Geoffrey hopes that his time with the Accord-Bridge foundation will inspire other men to continue to champion humanitarian causes regarding the empowerment of women and girls.
