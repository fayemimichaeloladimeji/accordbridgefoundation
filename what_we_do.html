    WHAT WE DO

    OUR INITIATIVES
    TEENPRENEUR PROGRAMME

    This initiative provides personalized support, mentorship, and training, focusing on innovation, goal-setting, and tenacity for girls. The project aims to guide young girls as they transition from school to becoming small-business entrepreneurs.

    Through training and mentorship, we empower girls to pursue their dreams, fostering positive change not only in their lives but also for their families, communities, and beyond.

    WOMEN IN AGRICULTURE PROGRAMME

    We are working to create “AGRIPRENEURS” out of women within specific ecosystems by providing them with the relevant skill set and funding to help them thrive in the farming industry and the preliminary processing of their farm produce – harnessing the agricultural value chains.

    LIVELIHOOD INITIATIVES

    Accord-Bridge foundation promotes and provides income-generating skills for less privileged women and girls in communities of Nigeria. Our vocational training and support are tailored to the beneficiary's needs. Our different livelihood skills under this initiative will ensure that women receive training in different vocations ranging from Cake making/decorations, soap making, shoe and bag making, gift-bags designs, Tailoring, etc., to enable them to generate income and break free from dependency and poverty. The project also includes community networks for the participants to share their experiences and inspire each other.

    MICRO-CREDIT SCHEME

    Because, collaterals in the form of landed property are most times needed for loans, the lack or limited access to property limits women’s access to credit. Under this scheme, our organization facilitates credits as start-up kits for small-scale businesses. We also provide follow-up support.
