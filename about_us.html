    Who We Are

    Accord-Bridge Foundation is a not-for-profit organization dedicated to the economic empowerment and personal development of less privileged women and girls in Nigeria. Registered with CAC Nigeria (CAC/IT/NO 101906), our work spans across the country, addressing the challenges of poverty and limited opportunities faced by this vulnerable segment of the population.

    With our main center in Port-Harcourt, we promote and provide economic opportunities, helping women and girls thrive. Our capacity-building initiatives are designed to enhance their socio-economic well-being, equipping them with the skills, confidence, and support network to drive change in their communities.

    Built on empathy, passion, and collaboration, Accord-Bridge Foundation empowers women and girls through our initiatives like the "Teenpreneur" and "Agripreneur," programmes alongside the livelihood and microcredit schemes. Together, we are building an ecosystem of change-makers across communities in Nigeria.

    OUR VISION

    To be a catalyst for a Nigeria where all women and girls from less privileged backgrounds enjoy full citizenship, earn a livelihood with dignity, and create wealth and value for all.

    OUR MISSION

    To equip less-privileged women and girls in Nigeria by providing them with skills and opportunities for economic independence.

    GOAL

    To empower 1,000 women and Girls annually.
