    Corporate Sponsorship

    Are you thinking of giving a donation through your organization or Foundation?

    Your in-kind support and, or cash donations will enable more women and girls from less-privileged backgrounds in communities of Nigeria to get access to economic resources, for a better livelihood with dignity. Should your organization be interested in partnerships to support our Livelihood and capacity-building initiatives, email us; info@abf.org.ng or call +234 8184427674

    Sponsor an Event

    You can help Accord-Bridge Foundation raise funds and promote community awareness by hosting a fund raiser! Organizing a fundraiser is a fun way to collaborate with us in our mission and, to support the different empowerment initiatives we provide for the less-privileged young girls and women across communities in Nigeria.

    Ideas include but not limited to; dance-offs, 5 a-side football/volleyball matches, fashion show/sales, comedy nights etc.

    While you choose on the event type, we can provide you with;
    • Promotional assistance through our social media handles and website.
    • Inspiring staff and volunteers to speak at your event.
    For more information, please contact us via: info@abf.org.ng or call, +234 818 442 7674.
